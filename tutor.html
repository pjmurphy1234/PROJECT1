Students from UW-Platteville's School of Education provide virtual volunteer tutoring to grade school and middle school students. The virtual tutoring sessions allow the UW students an opportunity to gain teaching experience while providing help to younger studens in a variety of subjects.

This program provides grade and middle school students a safe way to obtain additional help so many need to succeed in the new virtual classroom environment

Our program was featured in the News!
